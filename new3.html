Basic Chess info

Chess pieces are made up of 8 pawns, 2 rooks, 2 bishops, 2 knights,
1 queen, 1 king and below shows how much tiles each piece can move.

1.Pawn: 1 tile // 2 at start

2.Rook: All straight tiles in a lane

3.Bishop: All diagonal tiles n a lane

4.Knight: An L shape which travels three tiles up then 3 on right and left landing on the last tile of the 2 tile move

5.Queen: All Straight and diaganol tiles in a lane

King: Can move 1 tile in all direction
