the objective of the game is to catpure the opponents king using your own pieces to help
you do it although it sounds simple the game can become very complex with hundreds of millions

Benifits of playing Chess



  1. Helps create problem-solving skills

  2. Helps in building social skills as you play new people

  3. Improves thinking skills and speed as you have to formulate new plans to defeat the opponent with a time restraint

  4. Improves memory since chess is not a game of wits but also memorisation of varios patterns

  5. Helps create creativity skills by constantly making people form new attacks or defenses in the game
